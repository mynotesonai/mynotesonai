Surface Integrals

Single integrals are used to find the area under a single variable function f(x) over an interval [a, b], whereas the double ingrals are for finding the volumeunder a multivariable function f(x, y) over a region R = [a, b] x [c, d]

Line integrals are for finding the surface area beneath a line or curve in space between two points. Surface integrals are used for finding the volume beneath a surfave over a region.

Example Find the surface integral when S is the part of the plane $ z = 1 + 2x + 3y $ that lies above the rectangle [0, 3] x [0, 2]
